## Executive Summary

Align Capital Partners (ACP) is a **growth‑oriented, lower middle market private equity firm** that emphasizes **control investments** in **B2B companies** across four core sector families—**Software & Tech‑Enabled Services, Professional Business Services, Industrial Services, and Specialty Manufacturing & Distribution**—with investment teams in **Cleveland and Dallas**. ACP positions itself as a “true partnership” investor focused on “shared success,” combining **speed and certainty** in execution with a repeatable post‑close operating model. citeturn21view1turn3view4turn4view0turn43view0  

ACP’s *stated* target profile centers on companies with **$3–$15M EBITDA** (or **>$5M ARR for software**), **enterprise value up to $150M**, and an **equity check/investment size of ~$20–$60M**, primarily across **North America**. ACP highlights platform attributes like **recurring demand**, **niche leadership/differentiation**, and meaningful margin profiles (e.g., **30%+ gross margin / 15%+ EBITDA margin** in multiple sector criteria statements). citeturn1view0turn6view0turn35view1turn35view2turn36view0
