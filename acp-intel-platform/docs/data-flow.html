Every prospect gets a Conviction Score from 0 to 100
The Deal Qualifier takes each detected signal and measures it against six investment dimensions. Higher scores mean stronger alignment with the firm's acquisition criteria. Scores update in real time as new signals arrive, so the pipeline is always current.
The Six Dimensions
  • Sector Fit (20%)Does it operate in a core sector lane?
  • Management Quality (20%)Is the leadership team strong and continuable?
  • Growth Runway (15%)How much growth potential exists?
  • EBITDA Quality (20%)Are earnings sustainable and diversified?
  • Deal Complexity (15%)Is the process competitive or proprietary?
  • Competitive Process (10%)How many other bidders are involved?
Priority Tiers
HOT Score 75-100 — Strong conviction. Active pursuit, IC presentation.
WARM Score 50-74 — Growing interest. Detailed diligence underway.
NURTURE Score 25-49 — Early signals. Monitor and maintain relationship.
STRATEGIC Score 0-24 — Long-term target. Track for future opportunity.
Example
"Apex Compliance scores 93 (HOT) — $11.5M EBITDA in core Software & TES lane, recurring revenue moat with 118% net revenue retention, founder succession signal at 93% confidence."

Every deal becomes a financial model before the first meeting
The Value Modeler translates deal intelligence into PE return projections. It calculates entry valuation, models earnings growth over the hold period, and projects exit returns across three scenarios — giving the deal team a clear picture of risk and reward before committing resources.

The system gets smarter with every deal outcome
The Portfolio Monitor closes the loop. It tracks what happened with each prospect — did the deal close? Did the thesis hold? Did the value creation plan deliver? These outcomes feed back into the scoring model, so predictions get more accurate over time. Every quarter, the conviction weights are recalibrated.
